About us

And our company

The WebLegion is a ride share comparison web application. We compare the fare between rideshare providers Uber and Lyft for the similar routes, so you don't have to hop in between both the apps and re-type the locations. we also provide the user with the traffic status in the route along with the charts comparing the price of Uber and Lyft and that's why users love you application. we save user's precious time to get the best result. Disclaimer- WebLegion is not affiliated with any of the taxi service providers. Fares displayed are the estimated price displayed by the Uber and Lyft APIs. The estimates do not include toll or surcharges.
